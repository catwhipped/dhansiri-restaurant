Our Story

Dhansiri began as a dream to bring the rich, aromatic flavors of Bengali cuisine to our community. Our founder and executive chef, Kana Kamrun, grew up in a family of passionate cooks and has spent decades perfecting recipes passed down through generations. Today, we share these authentic dishes with you in a warm, welcoming atmosphere.
